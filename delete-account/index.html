Delete your Property Locker account

You can request deletion of your account and associated data stored with Property Locker. This page is provided so you can make a request even if you no longer have the app installed.

What will be deleted

  • Your account (email/login)
  • Your properties, sessions, photos, and generated reports stored in our cloud

What may be retained

Minimal records required for fraud prevention, security, or legal compliance (e.g., purchase receipts) may be retained for the legally required period.

How to request deletion

Email us from the address you used in the app with the subject line “Delete my Property Locker account”.

Email support@property-locker.com

Processing time

We typically complete deletion within 7 business days and will notify you when done.

Questions? Contact support@property-locker.com.
